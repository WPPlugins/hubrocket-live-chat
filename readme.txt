HubRocket is a powerful yet lightweight live chat software which will enable you to engage with your website visitors.

== Description ==
HubRocket was designed to make it easy for small - medium sized businesses to communicate with their website visitors. HubRocket can be customized to match your brand details from the widget appearance on your website, to automating customer invitations and more.

== Installation ==
1. Log in to the WordPress admin area
2. Click 'Plugins' located on the left side menu
3. Click 'Add New'
4. Search for 'HubRocket Live Chat'
5. Select 'Install' on the 'HubRocket Live Chat' plugin authored by 'HubRocket'
6. Once installed, click Activate
7. Navigate to your HubRocket admin area -> Settings -> Installations and copy the GUID code
8. Paste your GUID into the WordPress admin area and click 'Save'.
